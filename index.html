Welcome! I am a second year Public Policy PhD student at the University of Texas. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Mary Evans and Andrew Waxman. I am an applied economist working on topics of Environmental and Energy Economics, Industrial Organization and Economics of Regulation. My research focuses include:

  • Political Economy of Regulatory Enforcement
  • Structural Analysis of Electricity Markets
  • Efficient and Equitable Pricing of Electricity Bills
